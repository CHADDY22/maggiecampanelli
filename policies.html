Deposits
I REQUIRE A $150 NON-REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT IN ORDER TO RESERVE YOUR APPOINTMENT DATE & FLASH DESIGN (WHERE APPLICABLE). THE DEPOSIT WILL BE APPLIED TOWARDS THE TOTAL COST OF YOUR TATTOO AT THE CONCLUSION OF OUR APPOINTMENT.  

IF YOU NEED TO CANCEL YOUR APPOINTMENT WITHOUT RESCHEDULING A BACKUP DATE, FOR ANY REASON, YOUR DEPOSIT WILL BE FORFEITED (EVEN IF YOU PROVIDE 48 HOURS NOTICE). IF YOU WISH TO RE-INITIATE THE APPOINTMENT AT A LATER DATE THEN A NEW DEPOSIT WOULD BE REQUIRED.  

IF I DON’T RECEIVE A DEPOSIT WITHIN ONE WEEK OF YOU CONFIRMING THE DATE / TIME I OFFERED YOU, THE OPENING WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY FORFEITED & OFFERED TO OTHER INTERESTED PARTIES. YOUR APPOINTMENT WON’T BE SCHEDULED UNTIL A DEPOSIT IS RECEIVED!
Tattoos
I exclusively tattoo designs based on anime and manga. This includes anything from my "Flash" highlight on instagram, Direct scans/renderings of anime & Manga, official Renders from Japanese anime, manga, and games.

I Do not take on projects that aren't related to my usual style, or tattoos done by other tattoo artists.

Please familiarize yourself with the types of tattoos I take on by looking through my instagram.
Rescheduling
IF YOU CONTACT ME TO RESCHEDULE YOUR APPOINTMENT, THE ORIGINAL DATE & TIME WE HAD SCHEDULED WILL IMMEDIATELY UP FOR GRABS & NO LONGER AVAILABLE (THIS APPLIES EVEN IF I HAVEN’T RESPONDED TO YOUR RESCHEDULE REQUEST). PLEASE BE 100% SURE YOU WOULD LIKE TO RESCHEDULE PRIOR TO EMAILING ME AS YOU WILL NO LONGER HAVE THE ORIGINAL DATE & TIME RESERVED FOR YOU.

ANY REQUESTS FOR A TOUCH UP OUTSIDE OF THIS TIMEFRAME MAY BE DECLINED & A $185 TOUCH UP FEE WOULD BE ASSESSED. THIS ALSO APPLIES TO NUMEROUS TOUCH UP REQUESTS
